It's A Boy!

I was so happy and excited when I found out my baby was a boy. I always wanted a boy first and I knew Devin also wanted a boy.

He's Here!

The moment I saw his precious face I was in love. I've never seen anything so small and cute. Staying in the hospital for a week after was very hard and I was so ready to get out of there and bring Asher home!

6 Months Old

When Asher Turned 6 months old he was sitting up, eating in his high chair, and was starting to get teeth!

One Year

1 Year Old! Time flew by so fast! When Asher turned 1 he was walking with help, saying a couple words and had a few teeth.

19 Months

Asher is now 19 months, walking, talking and getting into everything! Asher loves being outside, he loves being rocked, and he loves peanut butter! I am so proud of him every day for how much he's growing and learning and I can't wait to see what happens next! I don't know where I would be without him, he has definitely changed my life for better and I couldn't imagine my life without him.

The Family!

I love our little family and I cant wait to watch it grow and spend the rest of my life with them! I am so blessed to have them in my life.
